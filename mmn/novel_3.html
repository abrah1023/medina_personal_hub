Top Tier Providence

ComedyCultivation
Synopsis

Being reincarnated in a cultivation world, Han Jue realizes that he can live his life like a video game. He can reroll his cultivation potential and connate providence. So, he spends 11 years rerolling for a good one… In pursuit of longevity, Han Jue decides to cultivate in a low-profile manner. A thousand years later, generations of cultivators have become history. That is when the Immortal Realm decides to clean up the mortal world. So, Han Jue finally has no choice but to take action. Then he realizes, deities and gods aren't that powerful after all!
